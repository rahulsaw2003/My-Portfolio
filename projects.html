Gesture Controlled Robocar

Developed a robo-car in which hand gestures can control the movement by sending the responses to the motor driver. I have worked on this project as a part of our course, along with other 2 members. Robotic vehicle controlled by hand movement mainly benefits the disabled person, as those people with hand gesture moves in the direction they want to go without pressing any buttons.
